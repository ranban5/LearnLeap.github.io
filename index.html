About Us

LearnLeap Foundation is a registered educational trust dedicated to empowering and transforming the lives of developmentally and socially vulnerable children in India by providing educational opportunities and training.

Education is undeniably a cornerstone of a child's development, fostering their intellectual, social, and emotional growth, and preparing them for a fulfilling life and a successful future.

We are registered under Educational Charitable Trust with 12A/80G certification, ensuring that your donations are tax exempt.

Project

Project: Empowering Education through NIOS

Background

Many teenage girls and children with special needs are unable to thrive in conventional schools. The National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers flexibility, but learners often require extra guidance, mentorship, and inclusive support. This project bridges that gap, ensuring every child has the chance to complete 10th and 12th grade education with confidence.

Objectives

  • Provide academic coaching for NIOS students (10th & 12th).
  • Support girl children’s education, breaking gender barriers.
  • Build an inclusive learning environment for children with special needs.
  • Strengthen life skills, confidence, and career readiness.

Who We Support

  • Teenage girls (14–19 years) enrolled in NIOS.
  • Children with learning disabilities, mild autism, ADHD, and other special needs.

What We Do

  1. Academic Support – subject tutoring, exam workshops, mock tests, and digital learning.
  2. Inclusive Learning – Individualized Education Plans, trained special educators, assistive tools.
  3. Girl Child Empowerment – mentorship, awareness on health & hygiene, parent/community sensitization.
  4. Life Skills & Development – communication, problem-solving, career counseling, extracurriculars.

Impact & Outcomes

  • Higher enrollment and retention of girls in NIOS education.
  • Improved academic performance and exam success.
  • Empowered special needs learners with inclusive support.
  • Young learners equipped with confidence, independence, and future readiness.

LearnLeap Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to advancing education and empowering communities. We are committed to transparency and accountability in our financial operations. Our organization has been granted tax exemption under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Benefits for Donors

Donors can claim tax deductions on their donations to LearnLeap Foundation under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The deductions are subject to the provisions of the Act and the rules framed thereunder.

Eligibility for Tax Deduction

To be eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G, donors must ensure that:

  • The donation is made in cash or by any other mode (e.g., cheque, demand draft, online payment).
  • The donation is made to LearnLeap Foundation directly or through an authorized fundraising agency.
  • The donation receipt is obtained from LearnLeap Foundation.

Donation Receipts

We issue donation receipts to all donors, which can be used to claim tax deductions. The receipt will include the following details:

  • Name and address of the donor
  • PAN of the donor (if available)
  • Amount donated
  • Date of donation
  • Registration number under Section 80G

Tax Exemption Certificate

We provide tax exemption certificates to our donors, which can be used to claim tax deductions. You can contact us to request a copy.
